## Arrays
Working with arrays
```swift
import Foundation

let arr = [1, 2, 3]
var fruits = ["apple", "banana", "cherry"]
fruits.append("date")

print("Array: \(arr)")
print("Fruits: \(fruits), Count: \(fruits.count)")
```
